Bio

I earned Ph.D. degree from Department of Information Engineering (IE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I'm fortunately working with Prof. Sherman S. M. Chow. My research interests:

  • Differential Privacy (DP). Its new notions and applications in AI (notably, NLP). My thesis is about a new paradigm for training and inference of large langugange models (LLMs);
  • Applied Cryptography. Searchable Symmetric Encryption (SSE) (e.g., shortest distance queries on encrypted graphs) and privacy-preserving ML via cryptographic tools (e.g., MPC).

Prior to that, I received M.Eng. in School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Wuhan University (WHU) in 2018, advised by Prof. Qian Wang, and B.S. in HongYi Class (or honor college) of Computer Science at WHU in 2015. In 2021, I did a summer intern at the Shield Lab of Huawei.
