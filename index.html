Bio

I'm currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student of the Department of Information Engineering (IE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I'm fortunately working with Prof. Sherman S. M. Chow. My research interests include Differential Privacy (DP) with its application in AI and Applied Cryptography (e.g., Searchable Symmetric Encryption).

Prior to CUHK, I received my M.Eng. in School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Wuhan University (WHU) in 2018, advised by Prof. Qian Wang, and B.S. in HongYi Class (university motto) of Computer Science at WHU in 2015.
